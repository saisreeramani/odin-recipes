New York Pizza!

Description

New York-style pizza has slices that are large and wide with a thin crust that is foldable yet crispy. This pizza is traditionally topped with mozzerla cheese and tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • 22 1/2 ounces bread flour, plus more for dusting (638g; about 4 1/2 cups)
  • 0.5 ounce granulated sugar (15g; about 1 1/2 tablespoons)
  • 0.35 ounce kosher salt (10g; about 1 tablespoon)
  • 0.35 ounce instant yeast (10g; about 2 teaspoons)
  • 15 ounces lukewarm water (415g; about 1 3/4 cups)(see note)
  • 3 tablespoons (45ml) extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 batch New York Style Pizza Sauce
  • 1 pound grated full-fat dry mozzarella cheese (454g; about 4 cups), placed in freezer for at least 15 minutes

Steps

  1. Prepare by making sure you gather all the required ingredients
  2. Let the dough sit in oven at 500 Farenheit for a couple of minutes
  3. Take the dough out and stretch it, don't toss it
  4. Top the dough with tomato sauce and cheese and other toppings that you prefer
  5. Bake it in the oven at 475 Farenheit for 10 minutes
  6. Enjoy your pizza
